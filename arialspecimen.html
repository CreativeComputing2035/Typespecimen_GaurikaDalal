About the font:

The Arial速 typefaceis one of the most widely used designs of the last 30 years. Drawn in 1982 by Monotype Imaging designers Robin Nicholas and Patricia Saunders for use in an early IBM速 laser printer, Arial has become a staple for textual content. While some believe Arial has its design roots in the Helvetica速 typeface, its foundation is actually in the Monotype Grotesque速 design, drawn at the turn of the last century.
